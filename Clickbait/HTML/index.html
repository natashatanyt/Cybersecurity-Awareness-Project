Clickbait & Misinformation

Introduction

Clickbait articles appeal to emotion, using a misleading headline to entice readers to click on articles. This allows them to generate revenue, usually at the expense of the quality of the article.

As a result, misinformation can spread. These articles are intentionally false, aimed at manipulating people’s perception of truth. By attempting to anger the readers, it encourages them to share articles, reaching a wider spread of people. This helps to spread misinformation and manipulate people’s perception of truth.

Misleading articles are becoming increasingly convincing, with some satirical articles being convincing enough to fool adults. Young children may not be able to identify these articles, sharing them with friends, spreading misinformation. This can damage their credibility and reputation amongst peers. Additionally, the ability to discern and identify truth from fiction is an important skill to have, which should be taught from a young age.

Avoiding Clickbait

  • Think before you click - Avoid clicking on headlines that shock you
  • Check the source - Some news sites are intentionally satirical
  • Read the article - Check the content before you share

Deepfakes

Introduction

Deepfakes are audio and/or visual content, edited to show someone’s face, body or voice to do something they have not done. These videos can be dangerous as they can spread lies, tricking the public.

They are increasingly realistic, becoming harder to differentiate from reality. Because of these videos, victims may be shown to be saying something controversial, and may result in them being doxxed.

By teaching your child about deepfakes, it reduces the chances that they are fooled, and prevents them from spreading misinformation.

Signs of Deepfakes

  • Glitches - rippling/blurring around the neck, eyes, or mouth, and is often more obvious when the person moves or blinks
  • Content - If the content of the video is different from how the person generally behaves, it may be a deepfake
  • Out-of-sync Audio - Lips movements do not match the audio
