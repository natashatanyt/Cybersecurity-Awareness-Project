How can you check if an article is false?

A

Use common sense

B

Check the source

C

Search for similar headlines from reputable news outlets

D

All of the above

Outlandish and unbelievable headlines are likely to be fake. Satirical outlets may produce credible articles, but will explicitly state they create fictional articles in the "About Us" section. If you are still unsure, search for similar articles from trusted outlets.

Which of these statements about clickbait is false?

A

Clickbait articles often include misinformation

B

Clickbait articles help companies earn money from advertisers

C

Clickbait articles are well-written and have credible sources

D

Clickbait articles can spread fake news

Clickbait articles disregard quality and prioritize generating revenue. As their goal is to get readers to spread the article, they may embellish content in a way that encourages readers to share the article, spreading misinformation.

What are signs that the image/video is a deepfake?

A

The background looks weird

B

The audio and video are out-of-sync

C

The video looks pixelated and blurry

D

All of the above

Even if you are aware of the signs of deepfakes, it can be difficult to spot fictional videos and images. Apart from these signs, consider whether the content matches the known opinions of the speaker.
