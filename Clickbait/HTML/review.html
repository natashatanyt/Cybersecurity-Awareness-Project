How can you check if an article is false?

A

Use common sense

B

Check the source

C

Search for similar headlines from reputable news outlets

D

All of the above

If the headline seems outlandish and unbelievable, it is likely to be fake, attempting to entice you to click on the article. Sometimes, the website that publishes the articles is satirical, intentionally publishing misleading headlines, which others believe to be true. If you are still unsure, search for similar articles from other reputable outlets. If it is also reported by them, it is likely to be true.

Which of these statements about clickbait is false?

A

Clickbait articles often include misinformation

B

Clickbait articles help companies earn money from advertisers

C

Clickbait articles are well-written and have credible sources

D

Clickbait articles can spread fake news

Clickbait articles are written with the intent to entice readers to click on the article in order to generate revenue from advertisers. Often, these articles have little regard for how well-written the content is, as the intent is not to report but to make money. Thus, it may also include misinformation. As the intent is for readers to share the article, fake news and misinformation can be spread when shared.

What are signs that the image/video is a deepfake?

A

B

C

The video looks pixelated and blurry

D

All of the above

Even if you are aware of the various signs, it can still be very hard to spot whether videos and images are fake. Apart from these signs, you should also consider whether the content of videos matches the known public opinions of the speaker.
