What information is leaked when you are doxxed?

A

Your address

B

Your phone number

C

Photographs of yourself

D

All of the above

Doxxing releases a lot of personal information about you, which gives others the ability to identify who you are, where you live, and how to contact you. Other information can include incriminating secrets that you do not wish to share. With this information, it becomes easier for the attacker and others to stalk and harass you.

What should you do if your friend has been doxxed?

A

Report the post

B

Inform your friend that they have been doxxed

C

Do not share the post

D

All of the above

If you have been doxxed, you should take screenshots and keep evidence, report the post, and make a police report. This is important as doxxing is illegal, and collecting evidence can help to prove your case before the post is taken down.

How do you prevent yourself from being doxxed?

A

Set your social media privacy settings to the highest possible

B

Use the same username on all platforms

C

Publicly share information about yourself online

D

None of the above

Limiting the information that is publicly available, it reduces the chance that attackers can connect your various accounts together, making it harder for them to build a profile with your information to publish it online.

Which of these is an example of doxxing?

A

Posting a video of a dangerous driver to warn people to drive safely

B

Posting contact information of a celebrity, encouraging others to threaten/attack them

C

Publishing information of a missing person to try and find them

D

None of the above

Doxxing requires the intent of causing harm or distress to the victim. Posting the video of the driver or the information of a missing person is not doxxing, as the intent is to warn others to be careful when driving, or to help locate a missing person. Posting the information about a celebrity and encouraging others to harm them is doxxing.

Which of the following are reasons why people are doxxed?

A

Posting racist comments online

B

Defeating others in a game

C

Speaking out about controversial topics

D

All of the above

As a method of enacting revenge due to disagreements, attackers may choose to doxx others. This can happen due to a minor disagreement, or under the guise of social justice, doxxing those who say controversial statements, to scare them into silence.
