What information should you never tell strangers?

A

Your home address

B

Your full name

C

Photos of yourself

D

All of the above

Personal information should never be shared with strangers online, especially if it can be used to identify you. Information that you can share should be something that is common, such as your favorite food, color, or music that you listen to.

How can you ensure that the person online is who they claim to be?

A

They send pictures of themselves

B

You cannot tell

C

You have the same online friends

D

They are nice

Unless you have met them in person, you can never be sure that the person online is who they say they are. Being online allows people to be anonymous, and they can pretend to be whoever they want.

What is the best way to protect my data online?

A

Do not use the internet

B

Accept anyone who requests to be my friend

C

Turn on the highest privacy settings

D

Do not post anything

It is impossible to stay off the internet and most people with social media accounts use it to keep in contact with their friends. By setting your account to the highest privacy settings and accepting those you know, it limits who sees your posts.

When is it okay to meet up with an internet friend?

A

With a trusted adult in a public place

B

By yourself in a public place

C

At their house

D

At your house

If you do want to meet up with an online friend, you should never meet up at a private or secluded area. You should ask a trusted adult to accompany you as it ensures that if something goes wrong, they will be able to help.
